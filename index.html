FUN, MAGIC, & MONSTERS

Welcome to the very beginning of the trading card game, Elemental Creatures. My name is Justin Hartsock and I am the creator and sole designer of the game.

Trading cards, tabletops, and board games have been an interest of mine for as long as I can remember and creatingmy own version of this form of media has been a personal goal now for close to two decades. My current goal is simply to create a fun and strategic game of my own imagination that resembles a professionally done product that is even more enjoyable to play.

The intent for this site is to invite and share with my community of fellow trading card enthusiasts as I continue to grow and optimize every aspect of Elemental Creatures.

If you'd like to reach out for any reason, I appreciate any interest for my work- my email is ElementalCreaturesTCG@gmail.com and will get back to you as soon as I can
